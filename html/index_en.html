Welcome to our website
Welcome to Computational Mechanics Laboratory at Shinshu University.
      We develop topology optimization, which is the most flexible structural optimization, and study structural design that exhibits new and extraordinary functions. We propose effective solutions to difficult properties that appear in structural optimization such as multi-modality, interdependency between design variables, multi-dimensional space of design variables, and high ratio of physical property value. By applying them to cutting-edge physical problems such as cloaking and illusion physics, we demonstrate its effectiveness and aim at advanced control of wave systems (optical/electromagnetic, acoustic) and diffusive systems (thermal/electrical).
